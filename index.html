FAQs

What's the difference between Team, Site, and System licensing?
Team is for small groups (1-10 users) who want to build AI apps with limited student access. Site is for single schools or organizations with all staff and optionally all students. System is for district-wide or multi-site deployment with volume discounts.
What are Learning Services?
Learning Services are professional development and implementation support offerings that help organizations build AI literacy, create custom applications, and develop sustainable innovation practices. These can be added to any licensing tier.
Do you offer pilot pricing?
Yes. Most partners begin with a smaller pilot or cohort and receive 50–90% discounted pricing in Years 1–2 as adoption ramps. Contact us to discuss your pilot timeline and budget.
Is Playlab safe for students?
Yes. Playlab has been vetted by NYC Public Schools and California Community Colleges. We feature best-in-class AI safety with agentic moderation, content filtering, and full admin oversight.
